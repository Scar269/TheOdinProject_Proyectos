French Toast

This baked French toast recipe is as easy to make as it is to eat!


Baked French Toast Ingredients

These are the ingredients you’ll need to make this baked French toast recipe:

  • French bread: This recipe starts a loaf of French bread, cut into diagonal pieces.
  • Eggs: The base of the French toast custard is eight large eggs.
  • Milk and half-and-half: You’ll need milk and half-and-half to complete the custard.
  • Vanilla and cinnamon: Take the warm and cozy flavor up a notch with vanilla extract and ground cinnamon.
  • Butter: Butter lends richness and flavor.
  • Brown sugar and corn syrup: Sweeten things up with brown sugar and light corn syrup.

Directions

  1. Butter a 9x13-inch baking dish; arrange slices of bread on the bottom.
  2. Beat eggs, milk, cream, vanilla, and cinnamon together in a large bowl. Pour over bread slices, cover, and refrigerate overnight.
  3. The next morning, preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Meanwhile, combine butter, brown sugar, and corn syrup together in a small saucepan; heat until bubbling.
  4. Pour over bread and egg mixture.
  5. Bake in preheated oven, uncovered, for 40 minutes.
  6. Serve with berries and syrup.
